Your medspa has five-star reviews, a loyal client base, and a treatments menu that converts in the room.

But your website? It's probably costing you a client a week.

## The 5-Second Test

A prospective client finds you on Google Maps. They tap your link. They have five seconds before they decide whether to book or bounce.

In those five seconds, they're not reading. They're **feeling**.

Is this place premium? Is it clean? Does it feel like the kind of medspa that does things right?

Most medspa websites fail this test — not because they're ugly, but because they feel generic. Template-tier. Like every other clinic on the block.

## What "Generic" Actually Costs

Let's be direct: a website that doesn't convert is a tax on every dollar you spend on ads, every post you publish, every referral that comes your way.

If your site converts at 1% and a better site converts at 3%, you're paying triple for every client you acquire.

For a medspa doing $400k/year, that gap can mean **$80k–$120k in lost revenue** — booked by the clinic down the street with the better site.

## The Five Things That Kill Conversions

1. **Slow load time on mobile.** Over 70% of your traffic is mobile. Google penalizes slow sites. So does your bounce rate.
2. **No clear CTA above the fold.** "Welcome to our spa" is not a CTA. "Book your free consultation" is.
3. **Stock photography.** Clients can smell inauthenticity. Real photos of your space and your work convert better — always.
4. **No trust signals.** Reviews, before/afters, credentials, and provider bios. If they're buried or missing, you lose the sale.
5. **No schema markup.** This one is invisible to humans but critical for Google. It affects your local pack ranking directly.

## What a Celebrity-Quality Site Changes

When a site is fast, beautiful, and built around conversion — it doesn't just book more appointments. It attracts better clients. Clients who don't haggle on price. Clients who refer their friends.

It also positions you as the premium option in your market, which matters more than you think in a space where every clinic offers the same services.
